# Merchant Pig (Farm Animal)

Adds the Traveling Merchant's purple pig as a farm animal you can purchase from Marnie's Ranch. Haven't you always wanted to own him? The Merchant Pig produces the new **Gilded Truffle** item! With the Gilded Truffle you can place it in an Oil Maker and make the new **Gilded Truffle Oil** Artisan Good!

## ⬇ Produce ⬇

![Gilded Truffle](https://i.imgur.com/YUCbIpY.png) **Gilded Truffle** — The Merchant Pig's normal produce. A Gilded Truffle will spawn every 3 Days.

<details>
<summary>Spoiler</summary>

![Fez](https://i.imgur.com/Or3xmUK.png) **Fez** — When at max Hearts ![heart](https://i.imgur.com/NurlCFl.png) with your Merchant Pig, there is a 5% chance of him producing a Fez instead of a Gilded Truffle.

</details>

![Installation](https://i.imgur.com/ch2OUHQ.png)

1. Locate your Game Folder and open the Mods folder inside it.
2. [Download Latest Github Release](https://github.com/JigJagg22/Merchant-Pig-Farm-Animal-/releases/latest) or [Download Latest Nexus Release](https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/45095?tab=files)
4. Unzip the mod's zip file inside your Mods folder.
5. ENJOY!

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Uninstalling this mod from an ongoing save will cause any Merchant Pigs, Gilded Truffles, and Gilded Truffle Oils to show as missing items. It is recommended to sell or remove all mod items before uninstalling!

## Other Notes

- **To buy a Merchant Pig, you require a Deluxe Barn.**
- **To produce Gilded Truffles, your Merchant Pig needs to go outside on non-rainy days.**
- Gilded Truffle Oil takes 1 Day to make from a Gilded Truffle in an Oil Maker.
- This Mod Works with Ongoing Saves.
